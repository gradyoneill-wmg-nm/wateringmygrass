The Science

This is not magic.
It's frequencies.

Every session uses specific Hz frequencies with documented research behind them. No claims beyond what the science supports.

40Hz
Gamma — Focus + Cognitive Activation
MIT researchers (Iaccarino et al., 2016) demonstrated 40Hz gamma entrainment reduces amyloid plaques in Alzheimer's mouse models. Also associated with heightened attention and information integration in healthy adults.
Iaccarino et al., Nature, 2016 · MIT Picower Institute
7.83Hz
Schumann Resonance — Grounding
Earth's electromagnetic resonance frequency. Preliminary research suggests entrainment at this frequency may support parasympathetic nervous system activity and reduce stress markers.
Schumann, 1952 · Pobachenko et al., Biophysics, 2006
10Hz
Alpha — Calm Focus
Alpha waves (8-12Hz) are the brain's signature of alert relaxation. Elevated in experienced meditators. Associated with flow states and reduced anxiety.
Neurofeedback research · Klimesch et al., Brain Research Reviews, 1999
