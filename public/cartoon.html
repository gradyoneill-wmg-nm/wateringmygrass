THE CHARACTERS
🐀
RAT
THE DREAMER — IN THE CLOUDS
Lives on a floating cloud above the city. Always in meditation. Eyes closed. Monk robes, but with a Non Magic app open on a laptop beside him. Visible theta waves ripple from his head in concentric circles.When things go wrong around him, his HRV monitor (strapped to his wrist) keeps going UP. He is the Tom — chaos happens TO him. He doesn't notice.
SIGNATURE GAG: Car crashes into him during meditation. He levitates slightly from impact. HRV: still perfect. Opens one eye. Nods approvingly. Closes eye.
🦟
ROCKBIRD
THE SCIENTIST — IN THE LAB
Manic, brilliant, tiny. Lab coat perpetually singed. Goggles pushed up. His entire building IS a tuning fork — it vibrates when he cranks his machines. Walls covered in frequency equations. Clipboard always in hand — he writes down the HRV of everyone nearby. Obsessed with Schumann resonance. His greatest discovery is always ONE more experiment away.
SIGNATURE GAG: Cranks frequency machine to 528Hz. A Wall Street banker stumbles in. Exits crying and hugging pigeons. Rockbird: furious scribbling in clipboard. Pure data.
🪳
ROACH
THE EVERYMAN — WATERING HIS GRASS
Just wants to water his tiny patch of grass in the city. That's it. Drags a comically large watering can through the chaos of Rat's serenity and Rockbird's experiments. Gets pulled into everything. Represents the audience — skeptical, curious, eventually convinced by the data. His grass is the moral barometer of the show. It grows when the frequencies are right.
SIGNATURE GAG: His grass grows visibly larger every time he passes Rockbird's lab. He never notices why. He just keeps watering.
