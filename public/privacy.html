PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Non Magic ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Non Magic mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information You Provide: Account information (email address and password). Voice data (processed for speech-to-text, not stored after processing). Session preferences, frequency selections, and feeling scores.

Health Data: Heart Rate Variability (HRV) from Apple Watch or connected wearables via Apple HealthKit, with your explicit permission. We do NOT access HealthKit data without opt-in consent. Health data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for purposes unrelated to your meditation experience.

Automatically Collected: Device type, OS version, app version. Anonymous usage analytics via PostHog. Crash reports via Sentry.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

To personalize your meditation sessions. To display HRV trends and session history. To process voice input for AI session calibration. To process subscription payments through RevenueCat (we do not store payment card details). To improve the App.

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers: OpenAI (voice transcription), ElevenLabs (voice generation), Anthropic (emotional analysis), RevenueCat (subscriptions), PostHog (analytics), Sentry (error reporting).

4. HEALTHKIT DATA

HealthKit data is not used for advertising or marketing. HealthKit data is not sold to third parties. HealthKit data is not shared without your explicit consent. HealthKit data is stored only on your device and in your secure account.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us. You may revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through device settings. You may delete your account by contacting privacy@nonmagic.com.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
